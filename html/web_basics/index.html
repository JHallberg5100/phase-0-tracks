    Carnivals I enjoy

  1. County fair

      Deep fried stuff everywhere

      Often little concern for safety

      Sometimes have mirror houses

      Classless in an enjoyable way most of the time

  2. World Fair

      Also called Columbian Exposition, which is pretty cool

      They gave birth to the ferris wheel, which I still enjoy

      Oftentimes used as a way to show off, at least before the Olympics

      The White City was pretty cool. My last office had pictures of the exhibits all over

  3. Traveling carnivals

      They have those spinny things

      Provide a place for stray children to run away to

      Also provide fodder for murder mysteries

      Sadly I have yet to see a man in a lion pelt lift a dumbell in one hand.
